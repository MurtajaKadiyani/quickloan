# FastFinance India — Policy and Compliance Guide

FastFinance India is a registered Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). This document describes FastFinance's lending practices, customer data policy, and complaint process.

---

## RBI Fair Practices Code for NBFCs

FastFinance India follows the RBI's Fair Practices Code for NBFCs, which requires transparent, non-discriminatory, and customer-friendly lending practices.

**Loan application and processing:**
FastFinance will acknowledge all loan applications and provide a written explanation if an application is rejected. Rejection reasons are shared in writing within 7 working days of a decision. FastFinance does not discriminate on the basis of gender, religion, caste, or community in lending decisions. Eligibility is determined solely by financial criteria (income, CIBIL score, age, and employment type).

**Interest rate and fee disclosure:**
The applicable interest rate and all fees (processing fee, prepayment charge, foreclosure charge) are disclosed in writing before loan disbursement. No fee is charged without prior written communication. QuickLoan will tell you the applicable rate based on your CIBIL score and product -- the final rate is confirmed in your sanction letter.

**Disbursement and repayment:**
Loan amounts are disbursed directly to the borrower's bank account. The repayment schedule, EMI amount, and total interest payable are provided in the loan agreement before signing. Borrowers may request a revised repayment schedule after disbursement; this is subject to processing.

**Recovery practices:**
FastFinance follows RBI guidelines on loan recovery. Recovery agents must identify themselves and carry authorisation letters. All recovery communications are in writing. FastFinance does not authorise recovery agents to contact borrowers outside the hours of 8 AM to 7 PM.

## Credit Bureau Consent

FastFinance India accesses your credit report from CIBIL (TransUnion CIBIL Limited) and/or Experian as part of the loan pre-qualification and final approval process. By using QuickLoan for a pre-qualification query, you are not authorising a credit bureau enquiry. A formal credit bureau enquiry (which may affect your CIBIL score) is initiated only when you submit a complete loan application with documents. QuickLoan's pre-qualification is based on the CIBIL score you provide and does not trigger a bureau enquiry.

---

## Joint Applications and Co-Applicants

QuickLoan handles pre-qualification for individual applicants only. Joint applications (spouse co-applicant, parent-child, business partners) and cases requiring combined income assessment are handled at the branch stage, not during online pre-qualification.

If you wish to apply jointly:
- Contact any FastFinance branch for a joint application form
- Both applicants must be present for KYC verification at the branch
- Combined income eligibility is assessed by a Loan Officer, not by QuickLoan

QuickLoan can provide individual pre-qualification for each applicant separately, but cannot model combined eligibility for joint applications.

## Data Privacy -- DPDP Act 2023

FastFinance India complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

**What data we collect:**
When you use QuickLoan, we collect the information you provide in the conversation (income, age, employment type, CIBIL score, loan amount requested). This information is used solely to pre-qualify your loan enquiry. We do not store conversation content beyond the current session unless you submit a formal loan application.

When you submit a formal loan application, we collect: full name, date of birth, PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, income documents, and bank statements. This data is retained for the duration of your loan relationship plus 7 years after closure, as required by RBI record-keeping guidelines.

**Your rights as a data principal:**
Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a summary of personal data FastFinance holds about you
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate personal data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of personal data where retention is not legally required
- **Grievance redressal:** Raise a complaint with FastFinance's Data Protection Officer

To exercise any of these rights, write to: dpo@fastfinance.in or visit any FastFinance branch.

**Data sharing:**
FastFinance shares your data with credit bureaus (CIBIL, Experian) during formal application processing, and with RBI as required by law. We do not sell customer data to third parties.

## Complaint Process

**Step 1 -- Branch or app complaint:**
Raise your complaint at any FastFinance branch or via the FastFinance app. You will receive a complaint reference number within 24 hours. FastFinance aims to resolve complaints within 7 working days.

**Step 2 -- Nodal Officer:**
If your complaint is not resolved within 7 working days, or if you are unsatisfied with the resolution, escalate to the Nodal Officer: nodal@fastfinance.in. Resolution at this level: 10 working days.

**Step 3 -- RBI Ombudsman:**
If FastFinance does not resolve your complaint within 30 days, or if the resolution is unsatisfactory, you may approach the RBI Integrated Ombudsman Scheme (free service). File online at cms.rbi.org.in or call 14448.

QuickLoan can explain the complaint process but cannot raise a complaint on your behalf. Visit any FastFinance branch or use the FastFinance app to file a formal complaint.
